Tourist Attractions

Here are some beautiful and popular tourist spots in Malang. Click a card to learn more.

Mt. Bromo

Mount Bromo is one of East Java’s most famous natural attractions, located near Malang. Known for its breathtaking sunrise views, sea of sand, and active volcanic crater, it offers a truly unique landscape that feels otherworldly and unforgettable for visitors. Many travelers also enjoy cultural experiences with the local Tenggerese people, who consider the mountain sacred.

Museum Angkut

Museum Angkut in Malang is a unique transportation-themed museum that showcases vehicles from different eras and countries. With its immersive displays and life-sized sets, it feels like traveling through time and across the world. It’s a fun mix of education and entertainment, making it a favorite spot for both families and tourists.

Jatim Park 3

Jatim Park 3 in Malang is a family-friendly theme park famous for its dinosaur-themed attractions and interactive rides. Visitors can explore life-sized prehistoric replicas, fun playgrounds, and modern entertainment zones. It combines education and leisure, making it an exciting destination for all ages.

Kampung Warna Warni

Kampung Warna Warni in Malang is a vibrant riverside village transformed with brightly painted houses and colorful murals. Walking through its alleys feels lively and full of charm, perfect for photos and cultural exploration. It has become a symbol of community creativity and a must-visit spot for tourists.
