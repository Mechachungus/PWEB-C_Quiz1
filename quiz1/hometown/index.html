My Hometown

A gallery of places from where I grew up.

Malang

Malang is a cool and charming city in East Java, Indonesia, known for its pleasant climate, colonial architecture, and rich cultural heritage, but most importantly it's where I grew up. Surrounded by mountains and tea plantations, it offers a mix of natural beauty, historical sites, and modern attractions. From delicious local cuisine like Bakso Malang to popular destinations such as Mount Bromo, Jatim Park, and colorful villages, Malang is a favorite getaway for both local and international travelers.
