Famous Local Food

Discover the delicious local cuisine from Malang

Bakso (Meatballs)

Malang meatballs are special because of their generous portions with various fillings, such as smooth meatballs, tendon meatballs, wontons, siomay, tofu, and vermicelli. Another characteristic is the broth, which is richer and more savory since it is cooked with bones and offal, and it is served with a variety of crispy fried items like fried meatballs and fried wontons to add texture and flavor.

Rujak Cingur

Famous Indonesian dish from East Java, consisting of a mix of tropical fruits and vegetables, such as young mango, pineapple, and water spinach, along with other ingredients like fried tofu, tempeh, and the unique ingredient "cingur," which is boiled beef snout

Putu Lanang

Putu is a traditional Indonesian and Southeast Asian sweet treat made from steamed rice flour, often flavored with pandan leaves and colored green. It is typically cylindrical in shape, formed by pressing the flour mixture into a bamboo tube, filled with a core of melted palm sugar, and then steamed until cooked.
